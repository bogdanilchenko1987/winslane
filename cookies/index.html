Cookie Policy for Winslane Lotto

Last Updated: April 6, 2025

This policy explains our use of cookies and similar technologies when you visit the Winslane Lotto website. Please read it carefully to understand the types of cookies we use, the data collected, and how we manage that information.

Cookies are small data files placed on your device (computer, smartphone, etc.) when you visit a website. They help the site remember your actions and preferences over time.

While cookies themselves generally do not contain personally identifiable information, the personal data we store about you may be linked to information collected through cookies. For details on how we protect your personal information, please see our Privacy Policy.

We do not store sensitive data like passwords, payment details, or email addresses in cookies.

Interpretation and Definitions

The following capitalized terms have specific meanings:

  • Company, We, Us, or Our: Refers to Winslane Lotto.
  • Cookies: Small data files placed on your device when you visit our website.
  • You: Refers to the individual or entity accessing or using the Website.

How We Use Cookies

We use both session cookies (deleted when you close your browser) and persistent cookies (remain on your device after you go offline).

Types of Cookies We Use:

  • Necessary Cookies (Session Cookies): Essential for core website functions like security, account login, and navigation. Without these, requested services cannot be provided.
  • Cookie Consent Cookies (Persistent Cookies): Track whether you have consented to cookie usage.
  • Functional Cookies (Persistent Cookies): Remember your preferences (login details, language, etc.) to improve your experience across visits.
  • Performance and Analytics Cookies (Persistent Cookies, Third-Party Administered): Collect information about user interaction with the site (most visited pages, etc.) to help us optimize the website and user experience. This data may identify your device via a pseudonymous ID.
  • Targeting and Advertising Cookies (Persistent Cookies, Third-Party Administered): Analyze your browsing behavior to show you personalized and relevant advertisements.

Your Choices Regarding Cookies

You can manage your cookie preferences at any time by adjusting your browser settings to refuse cookies and delete existing ones. Please be aware that declining cookies may impact website functionality and personalization.
